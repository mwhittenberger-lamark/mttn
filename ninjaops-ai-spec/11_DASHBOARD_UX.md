# Dashboard UX

## Navigation
Overview, Projects, Research, Ideas, Drafts, Calendar, Social, Media, Integrations, Settings, Audit Logs.

## Overview
Cards for active projects, ideas awaiting review, articles awaiting review, scheduled posts this week, failed jobs, integration issues. Include recent activity and upcoming content.

## Project setup
Fields: project name, website URL, industry, service area, target audience, brand voice, timezone, default publish hour, auto-publish toggles.

## Integrations
Cards for Sanity, Meta, Google Ads, TikTok, LinkedIn. States: not connected, connected, needs reauth, missing permissions, no account selected, error. Actions: connect, reconnect, test, disconnect, select accounts.

## Research runs
List status, pages crawled, keywords found, ideas generated, started/completed timestamps, errors. Actions: start, view, retry, cancel.

## Ideas
Table filters by status, intent, funnel stage, score, cluster. Detail page shows title, keyword, angle, competitor gap, CTA, internal links, rationale, approve/reject/edit.

## Briefs/articles
Article editor supports title, slug, excerpt, meta fields, Markdown body, featured image preview, QA checklist, approval, push to Sanity, schedule.

## Calendar
Month/week view showing blog, social, and video posts by status. Allow rescheduling, opening, approval.

## Jobs/audit
Job detail shows job name, entity, status, attempts, timestamps, error, retry. Audit log filters by user/action/entity/date.

## UX rules
Use plain labels: Generate 50 ideas, Review draft, Push to Sanity, Schedule for Monday, Connect LinkedIn, Needs reauthorization. Show toasts when jobs are queued and clear errors with retry actions.
