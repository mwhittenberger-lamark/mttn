# Mike the Tech Ninja Service Page Wireframes

These are SEO-oriented wireframes for the six core service pages. Each page should be built as a focused local service landing page, not a thin duplicate of the homepage. The structure should help small business owners understand the service, recognize the problem, see what is included, and book a Free Digital Gut Check.

## Shared Service Page Strategy

### Shared Primary CTA

**Book a Free Digital Gut Check**

### Shared Secondary CTA

**Ask About This Service** or **See How This Could Work for My Business**

### Shared Local SEO Framing

Use Triangle language naturally in the hero, intro, local proof section, and FAQ. Rotate Raleigh, Cary, Durham, Chapel Hill, Apex, Morrisville, Holly Springs, Wake Forest, and Fuquay-Varina where it makes sense, but avoid stuffing city names repeatedly.

### Shared Page Structure

1. Hero
2. Problem / pain section
3. What this service helps with
4. What is included
5. How Mike makes it practical for small businesses
6. Local Triangle relevance
7. Process
8. Related services
9. FAQ
10. Final CTA

### Shared Internal Links

Each service page should link to:

Homepage

Free Digital Gut Check landing page

Related service pages

Relevant blog posts

Contact / booking section
